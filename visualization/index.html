The shapes of characters today stem from a long history of human culture. The goal of this visualization is to enable exploration of the visual themes of scripts in their historical and geographical context. On the left there is an evolutionary tree of the scripts leading up to a heatmap mapping the similarity scores between these scripts, as calculated by our algorithm. On the right there is a comparison information area. A secondary page presenting all the clusters can be found here. More information on each section can be found on the bottom. This project was done as part of a Thesis for a Master in IT at the Harvard Extension School, under the advising of Professor Hanspeter Pfister and Alexander Lex.
